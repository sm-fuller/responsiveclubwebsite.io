PhotoGens

About Us

Launched in 2018, PhotoGens is a non-profit organization, dedicated to promoting the art and science of photography.

We have regular workshop meetings, we go out on photo safaris and we hold an annual event that showcases the wealth of photographic talent in our club.

PhotoGens is open to all, whatever level of experience or knowledge. No qualifications are required to join. The organization provides opportunities to share knowledge, meet and learn from other photographers and to develop photographic interests and skills.

Membership is open to anyone with an interest in photography. Click Join to fill out the online membership form and submit your membership dues.
